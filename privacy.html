Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-09-01

Introduction

At Elmidor Group, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Please read carefully to understand your rights and how we safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details (name, email, phone) when you fill out forms or create accounts.
  • Profile information, messages, uploads, or any content you submit to our platform.
  • Billing and payment details when you make a purchase (processed securely by third-party providers).

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken, timestamps).
  • Device and browser data (IP address, user agent, operating system).
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies section below).

2. How We Use Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services and workflows.
  • Communicate with you about products, updates, and support (with your consent where required).
  • Process payments, subscriptions, and donations securely.
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We and trusted partners use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and marketing. You may manage cookies via your browser settings or through our cookie banner (where available).

Common third-party services we use: Google Analytics, Facebook/Meta Pixel, Stripe (for payments). Always check our integrations page for the latest list of tools we use.

4. Sharing & Disclosures

We may share your data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, payments, CRM, communications).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
  • Business partners when you opt in to integrated services.
  • Successors in case of business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition) with notice.

We do not sell personal data under any circumstances.

5. Data Security & Retention

We apply industry-standard measures (encryption, access controls, monitoring) to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorized access, contact us immediately.

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Restrict or object to our processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise rights, email us at: support@elmidorgroup.com.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence. We rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to ensure your data remains protected.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the local minimum age of consent). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the effective date and provide notice where required. Continued use of our services means you accept the updated Policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data rights, contact us at: support@elmidorgroup.com.

Privacy FAQ

Why does Elmidor Group collect personal data?

We collect data to provide our services, improve performance, and deliver personalized experiences for our users.

Do you sell data to advertisers?

No. We never sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted service providers under strict agreements.

Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time unless we are legally required to keep it (e.g., for tax or regulatory purposes).

How long do you keep my data?

We retain your information only as long as it is necessary for the purposes described in this Policy and to comply with applicable laws.
