Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you interact with our website, products, demos, or services (including no-code workflow automation tools, demos, and community resources). We wrote this policy in clear, straightforward language so you can understand your rights and how we handle your data.

If you have questions or want help exercising your rights, contact our Privacy Team at support@elmidorgroup.com or visit our Contact page.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you use our site or services, you may give us:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number).
  • Profile information and company details (when you create an account or register for a demo).
  • Form submissions, messages, feedback, and comments (for support, demos, or testimonials).
  • Payment details (when you buy a paid service). Payments are processed by trusted third-party payment providers; we never store full card data on our servers.
  • Files or uploads you submit (documents, screenshots, or attachments when requested).

Information we collect automatically

We and our service providers automatically collect technical and usage data such as:

  • Usage data (pages visited, clicks, time on page, form submission timestamps).
  • Device and connection information (IP address, browser type and version, operating system, screen size).
  • Cookie and similar tracking data (see Cookies & Tracking below).

Third-party sources

We may also receive data about you from third parties (for example, analytics providers, social networks, or integrations such as Tally, Airtable, or Google) and combine that information with data we collect directly.

2. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services, demos, and automation templates.
  • Process form submissions, respond to inquiries, and deliver customer support.
  • Send transactional messages and confirmations (for example, demo sign-up confirmations or receipt emails).
  • Personalize your experience and recommend relevant resources or automation guides.
  • Secure our systems, detect fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations and requests from law enforcement, when required.

We only process your data where we have a lawful basis (consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests, or legal obligation).

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, understand usage, and deliver tailored content or ads. You can control most cookies through your browser settings or via our cookie banner (if shown).

Examples of tools we use: Google Analytics (analytics), Google Tag Manager, Facebook/Meta Pixel (marketing), and Stripe (payments).

To change cookie preferences, use your browser privacy settings or the cookie controls on our site. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Sharing & Disclosures

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, email delivery, payment processors, CRM and automation platforms (for example, Tally, Airtable, Google Workspace, Stripe, and messaging providers). These partners process data on our behalf under contracts that require appropriate safeguards.
  • Business partners: when you opt into integrations or joint services.
  • Legal authorities: if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Successors: if our business is merged, sold, or transferred; we will notify you where required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls, monitoring, and secure data storage) to protect your information. While we work to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data retention: We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or to meet legal obligations. Typical retention examples:

  • Account information: retained while your account is active plus a reasonable period (e.g., 2 years) for backup and legal purposes.
  • Customer support and message logs: retained to maintain service quality (commonly 1‚Äì3 years depending on the content and legal needs).
  • Transactional records (invoices, receipts): retained for tax and accounting obligations as required by law.

If you want more detail about retention for a specific data type, contact us at support@elmidorgroup.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your personal data (subject to legal or contractual exceptions).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Receive a copy or a portable format of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please submit a request to support@elmidorgroup.com or use our Data Request form. We will verify your identity before processing requests and respond within applicable legal timelines.

7. International Transfers

Elmidor Group is a global organization and may transfer your personal data to countries outside your country of residence. When we do, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses, data processing agreements, or other legal mechanisms required by applicable law to protect your data.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under the local minimum age). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child, we will delete it as soon as possible. If you believe we may have information about a child, contact us.
