General Introduction

Elmidor Group provides a support and transparency space for everyone interested in joining our future. You can choose the category that fits you best: as a client, collaborator, supporter, or even volunteer. Each section has a short intro explaining its goal, plus a button to fill out the relevant form.

FAQ

What is an “automated workflow”?

A predefined set of steps connecting tools, templates, and notifications to make processes run automatically (e.g., scheduling, CRM, reminders, request sorting, etc.).

Are there free options?

Yes, there are free pilots for some workflows; others depend on scale and usage.

How can I become a partner?

Go to Associate Partner, fill out the form, and our team will reach out for next steps.
