What is it?

This solution is an automation workflow that connects online forms (Tally) with a database (Airtable), sends email notifications, manages contacts in Google Contacts, creates events in Google Calendar, and finally sends messages on WhatsApp through an HTTP API. Every form submission becomes a record, a confirmation message, or an appointment ‚Äî all without manual work.

Who is it for?

This solution is designed for:

  • Small businesses and entrepreneurs receiving requests or bookings online.
  • Service agencies (health, education, consulting) that need to confirm appointments instantly.
  • Sales teams who want to follow up with leads and communicate instantly on WhatsApp.
  • Startups or organizations seeking to improve customer response times with automated workflows.

How does it work? (6 simple steps)

  1. Trigger: A user submits a Tally form.
  2. Store data: Responses are automatically recorded in Airtable.
  3. Notification: A confirmation email is sent to the client/team.
  4. Contact management: Google Contacts creates or updates the contact (avoiding duplicates).
  5. Scheduling: An event is created in Google Calendar if needed.
  6. WhatsApp communication: An HTTP request triggers a WhatsApp API (Twilio / WhatsApp Cloud API / 360dialog, etc.).

Which service layers does it cover?

  • Data Collection: Tally (online forms).
  • Storage & Retrieval: Airtable (database).
  • Contact Management: Google Contacts.
  • Scheduling: Google Calendar.
  • Communication: Email + WhatsApp via HTTP API.
  • Connectivity & Interoperability: HTTP requests for external APIs.

Key Benefits

  • Instant response: Clients receive a WhatsApp message the moment they submit the form.
  • Efficiency: Eliminates manual work, reduces human errors.
  • Effortless tracking: All data centralized in Airtable for reporting and follow-up.
  • Personalization: Messages can include client names, appointment dates, and dynamic details.
  • Scalability: The workflow adapts easily to multiple forms, campaigns, or business branches.
  • Compliance & Traceability: All actions are logged, useful for audits and customer support.
