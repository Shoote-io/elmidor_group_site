Elmidor Group Workflow Automation helps you collect, organize, and respond to customer requests without manual data entry. It integrates Tally forms, Airtable CRM, Email notifications, Google Contacts & Calendar, and the WhatsApp API into one seamless process.

By automating repetitive tasks, you save time, reduce errors, and deliver a faster, more reliable customer experience.

Who Benefits from Workflow Automation?

This solution is ideal for teams and professionals who want to collect and respond to client requests efficiently:

  • Small businesses & online stores managing daily orders
  • Coaches, consultants & service agencies that need streamlined booking and follow-up
  • Sales & marketing teams seeking a centralized hub to track leads
  • Startups & NGOs improving workflows without building custom software

How the Workflow Automation Works (6 Steps)

  1. Customer submits a form via Tally.
  2. Data is instantly stored in Airtable.
  3. A personalized confirmation email is sent.
  4. Contact details sync with Google Contacts.
  5. An appointment or event is scheduled in Google Calendar.
  6. An instant WhatsApp message is triggered via the API.

Service Layers of the Automation

  • Data Collection: Tally forms
  • Database & CRM: Airtable
  • Contact Sync: Google Contacts
  • Scheduling: Google Calendar
  • Messaging & Follow-up: Email & WhatsApp API
